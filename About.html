Hostels, IIIT Guwahati

The students of IIIT Guwahati are provided with the accomodation facilities in the campus itself. We have one boy's hostel and one girl's hostel in the campus.

ABOUT THE HOSTELS

Resident facilites are provided to students in one boy's hostel and girl's hostel. Presently all students are accomodated in these hostels. The hostels are graded roud the clock by able security staffs. The hostels are located in peaceful, clean, aesthetic environment in campus. Staying in the campus is particularly attractive because resident students have access to several facilities. Each Hostel is the institute is self-contained with amenities such as internet WiFi, an indoor games room, a dining hall mith mess, medical facilites, TV room, along with with food joints and canteen. The campus coommunity forms a close friendly group and where students make friend for life. Presently over 500 students are residing in the hostels. Students have acccess to Wi-Fi connectivity throughout the buildings. Medical Facilities are provided 24X7. Campus has a volleyball court, a badminiton court, a cricket ground.

Life at hostel

 

Beyond beds and books, hostel life grows up, is what they say. But at IIIT Guwahati, hostel life is more than just books and the hostel rooms. It's about sharing, caring, exciting events, and a home away from home with a new family. Here, hostel life is filled with fun and frolic as well as some frisk of study.

Most of the students have their first time stay away from their parents when they come to IIITG, but rest assured, there is nothing to worry about. The environment here is absolutely safe and secure. The community is very much caring and helpful. Starting to live in the hostel is like having unlocked a door to a treasure, a treasure of opportunities. IIIG community offers ample of diversified opportunities to aid student's personality development.

You can pursue whatever you want. You like dancing - there is dancing club, you like guitar - there is music club, you like robotics and exciting stuff - there is robotics club. Photography club, Literary club, Programming club, Entrepreneurship club (E-cell) and whatever your hobby is, you can always find peers with similar hobby in a club. You can dream whatever you want and chase it because sky is the limit. Within the first year, you may find yourself able to develop android apps or websites, just with the help of clubs.

Ragging

 

At IIIT Guwhati, ragging is completely prohibited. The Institute has zero tolerance towards ragging. In fact, when you start living at the hostels, you realize that the seniors are extremely helpful and are always there to guide their juniors with their experiences in various fields.

Living in the hostels with the seniors' help available at any hour truly makes the life at the hostels easier. You can learn a great deal from your seniors.
